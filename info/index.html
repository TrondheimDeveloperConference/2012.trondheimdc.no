To the speakers at Trondheim Developer Conference

Trondheim Developer conference approaches fast. This is the first year TDC is organized but not the last. The interest around the conference has been tremendous, and we were sold out one month before the conference starts. Now it's up to you to make the conference something the participants will never forget. We want the participants to go home with inspiration and high academic quality. We have carefully chosen the speakers at the conference with this in mind. We and the 250 participants are looking forward to your talks!

As you probably know, we have focused on four topics this year. The topics are frontend development, backend development, startups and agile. We have chosen to mix the different topics into four different tracks. This is done to balance the pressure on the different rooms at the conference venue. Sessions have a timespan of 30 minutes and it’s not possible to go over the time set. The keynote and the main lectures have 55 minutes available. For those of you holding lightning talks, two lightning talks share a 30 minute session. We need some minutes between, so each lightning talk can not take more than 13 minutes.

During the day we will have 4 breaks including lunch. In the breaks it’s possible for the attendees to mingle and talk to the fifteen different companies at the stands in the hall outside the conference rooms. We encourage the speakers to be available for questions in the hall area during the breaks.

Although this letter is in English, most of the talks at the conference will be held in Norwegian.

Speakers Lounge

For those who need a room to retreat to and does not stay at the venue, there will be a speaker's lounge where you can prepare before your talk and unwind after it. This will only be open for speakers and other certified personnel.

Traveling to Trondheim

If you are traveling by airplane, the easiest way to get to the conference hotel is by the Airport Express Coach. It leaves from Trondheim Airport to Trondheim every 10 minute. The Airport Express Coach (www.flybussen.no/trondheim) stops outside Rica Nidelven Hotel (www.rica.no/nidelven).

Party

After the conference is finished a party will be held at Søstrene Karlsen (www.sostrenekarlsen.no). We hope that the speakers have the opportunity to participate at the party. There will be one meal and one drink coupon for each attendee.

Contact information

If you need to get in touch with us, you can contact one of the members of the committee. You can find a list of the members at the end of this letter.

We are looking forward to seeing you at Trondheim Developer Conference 2012!
